Sinigang na Hipon

Description

Sinigang na hipon is a sour tamarind soup made with shrimps, string beans, long green chili peppers and radishes, and seasoned with fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2kg of shrimp
  • 2 packs of Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix
  • 1 medium white radish, sliced
  • 1 bunch of kangkong or spinach
  • 10 pieces of okra
  • 15 pieces of string beans sliced, into 2 inch length
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce or patis
  • 2 medium tomato sliced
  • 3 pieces of long green pepper
  • 6 cups of water
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Pour water in a cooking pot. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add onion, tomato, and radish. Cover and let boil for another 8 minutes.
  3. Add the shrimp. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add Knorr Sinigang Mix. Stir until it dilutes completely.
  5. Add long green pepper, spring beans, and the okra. Stir. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Put the kangkong stalks into the pot. Season with fish sauce and ground black pepper.
  7. Add the kangkong leaves. Cook for 1 minute.
  8. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve warm with rice.
